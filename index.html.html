Privacy Policy

Privacy should be the default, not an afterthought.

Last updated: 3 March 2026

🔒 Short version: TabMaster runs entirely in your browser. We collect no personal data, no browsing history and no tab information. Zero.

1. Who We Are

TabMaster is developed and operated by FKTech, Denmark. Contact us at support@gettabmaster.com.

2. Data We Collect

TabMaster does not collect, store or transmit any of the following:

  • Browsing history or open tab URLs
  • Tab titles or page content
  • Personal identifiable information
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Crash reports or analytics

All operations run entirely within your local browser. No data ever leaves your device.

3. Chrome Permissions We Request

  • tabs — to read and manage open tabs locally
  • tabGroups — to create and manage Chrome tab groups
  • storage — to save your preferences on your device only
  • alarms — to enable scheduled auto-suspension of inactive tabs

None of these permissions are used to transmit data externally.

5. Children's Privacy

TabMaster is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13 years of age.

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I install TabMaster? ▼
Go to the Chrome Web Store and search for TabMaster, or click Add to Chrome on this page. Click Add extension in the Chrome popup. The lightning bolt icon will appear in your toolbar.
Does TabMaster collect my browsing data? ▼
No. TabMaster runs 100% locally in your browser. We do not collect, store or transmit any browsing history, URLs or personal information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How does Suspend inactive tabs work? ▼
TabMaster uses Chrome's built-in tab discarding to unload inactive tabs from memory. The tab stays visible in your tab bar but uses no RAM. Clicking the tab reloads it instantly.
Will my tab groups be saved when I close Chrome? ▼
Chrome native tab groups persist if you have Continue where you left off enabled in Chrome settings. Full session save and restore is planned as a PRO feature in a future update.
Is TabMaster free? ▼
Yes, completely free. A PRO tier with auto-suspend schedules, session saving and cross-device sync is planned for the future. Core features will always remain free.
TabMaster is not working. What should I do? ▼
Try these steps: 1) Click the puzzle icon and pin TabMaster. 2) Right-click the extension icon and toggle it off and on. 3) Restart Chrome. Still stuck? Contact us at support@gettabmaster.com and we will respond within 24 hours.
How do I report a bug or request a feature? ▼
Use the Contact page or email support@gettabmaster.com directly. Feature requests are very welcome and the roadmap is shaped entirely by user feedback.
