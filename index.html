⚡ Smart Tab Grouping

One click groups all your open tabs by domain — 47 scattered tabs become 6 clean, colour-coded groups instantly. Chrome's native tab groups API makes them persist across sessions.

💤 Suspend Inactive Tabs

TabMaster uses Chrome's built-in tab discarding to unload inactive tabs from memory. Typical savings: 80% RAM freed. Tabs reload the moment you click them — nothing is lost.

🔍 Search All Tabs

Type anything into the search bar and find any open tab instantly by title or URL. Real-time filtering across every open tab — no more hunting through 50 favicons.

🗑 Duplicate Remover

Accidentally opened the same page three times? TabMaster detects and removes every duplicate tab with a single click, keeping the most recently active copy.

🔒 Permissions & Privacy

tabs
Reads open tab titles and URLs locally to enable grouping, search and duplicate detection. No data leaves your device.
tabGroups
Creates and names Chrome tab groups when you click "Group tabs". Required for the core grouping feature.
storage
Saves your preferences (e.g. auto-suspend on/off) to chrome.storage.local on your device only. Nothing is synced externally.
alarms
Schedules periodic auto-suspension of inactive tabs in the background. Runs locally — no network access.

✦ AI Video Summary

Get a concise bullet-point summary of any YouTube video in under 30 seconds. YTEnhancer fetches the video's captions and uses Claude AI to extract the key takeaways — no need to watch the full video first.

Requires a free Anthropic API key. Your key is stored locally on your device — never sent to FKTech servers.

⏭ Chapter Navigation

Videos with chapters show a clickable list in the panel. Click any chapter title to jump directly to that timestamp in the video — no scrubbing through the progress bar.

🚫 Spam Comment Blocking

Automatically hides comments matching spam patterns — "check my channel", "FIRST!!!", "sub 4 sub", and excessive emoji strings. Toggle on or off instantly from the filters tab.

🔍 Advanced Search Filters

Filter YouTube search results by sort order (relevance, date, views, rating), duration (under 4 min, 4–20 min, over 20 min) and upload date — all without navigating YouTube's own filter menus.

🔒 Permissions & Privacy

storage
Saves your Anthropic API key and feature preferences (spam blocking, hide counts) locally via chrome.storage.local. Nothing is sent to FKTech servers.
tabs
Detects navigation between YouTube videos so the panel resets and loads fresh chapter data for the new video.
youtube.com
Required to inject the floating panel UI onto YouTube pages and to read video description text for chapter timestamps. YouTube's own caption API is used for transcript data — no third-party transcript service is used.

FAQ
How do I install an extension? ▼
Go to the Chrome Web Store page for the extension and click "Add to Chrome". Confirm by clicking "Add extension" in the popup. The extension icon will appear in your Chrome toolbar.
Do FKTech extensions collect any data? ▼
No. All FKTech extensions run entirely locally in your browser. We never collect, store on external servers, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or usage information.
Why does TabMaster need host permissions? ▼
The host permission allows Chrome's tabs API to return full URLs for open tabs on all domains. Without it, URLs are hidden and TabMaster cannot group tabs by domain or detect duplicates. The extension never reads page content and never injects scripts into web pages.
Why does ChatOrganiser need access to AI platform sites? ▼
ChatOrganiser uses content scripts to read the conversation sidebar titles on ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and Perplexity. Only sidebar navigation text (conversation titles) is read — no message content, no account details, nothing else. All data stays on your device.
An extension is not working. What should I do? ▼
Try: 1) Click the puzzle icon and pin the extension. 2) Right-click the extension icon and toggle it off and back on. 3) Restart Chrome. Still stuck? Contact us at [email protected] with details and we respond within 24 hours.
Are the extensions free? ▼
Yes — core features are completely free. A PRO tier is planned for both extensions, adding power-user features like session saving and auto-suspend schedules. Core features will always remain free.
How do I report a bug or request a feature? ▼
Use the Contact page or email [email protected]. Feature requests are very welcome — the roadmap is driven entirely by user feedback.
