🔒 Short version: TabMaster runs entirely in your browser. We collect no personal data, no browsing history, and no tab information. Zero. Full stop.

1. Who We Are

TabMaster ("we", "our", or "us") is a Chrome browser extension developed and operated by FKTech. Our registered contact email is support@gettabmaster.com.

2. Data We Collect

TabMaster does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your browsing history or open tab URLs

All tab management operations occur entirely within your local browser using Chrome's built-in APIs. No data ever leaves your device.

3. Permissions We Request

TabMaster requests the following Chrome permissions:

None of these permissions are used to transmit data externally.

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I install TabMaster? ▼
Go to the Chrome Web Store, search for "TabMaster" or click "Add to Chrome" on this page. Click "Add extension" in the popup. The ⚡ icon will appear in your Chrome toolbar — click it to start organising your tabs.
Does TabMaster collect my browsing data? ▼
No. TabMaster runs 100% locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any browsing history, URLs, or personal information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How does the "Suspend inactive tabs" feature work? ▼
When you click "Suspend inactive tabs", TabMaster uses Chrome's built-in tab discarding to unload tabs that are not currently active from memory. The tab stays visible in your tab bar but uses no RAM. Clicking the tab reloads it instantly.
Will my tab groups be saved if I close Chrome? ▼
Chrome's native tab groups persist across sessions if you have "Continue where you left off" enabled in Chrome settings. Session saving and restoration is planned as a PRO feature in a future update.
Is TabMaster free? ▼
Yes — TabMaster is completely free to install and use. A PRO tier with advanced features (auto-suspend schedules, session saving, cross-device sync) is planned for the future. The core grouping, search, and suspend features will always be free.
TabMaster isn't working — what should I do? ▼
Try these steps: 1) Click the puzzle icon in Chrome and pin TabMaster. 2) Right-click the extension icon → "Manage Extension" → toggle off/on. 3) Restart Chrome. 4) If the issue persists, send us the details via the Contact page and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
How do I report a bug or request a feature? ▼
Use the Contact page or email support@gettabmaster.com. Feature requests are very welcome — the roadmap is shaped by user feedback.
